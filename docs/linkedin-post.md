## LinkedIn Post Draft

Most incident copilots are just fancy autocomplete for runbooks.
They look helpful until the third outage repeats the same failure mode.

I built Flashback Ops to fix that gap: every incident, fix, and postmortem outcome becomes retrieval signal for the next response.

What changed after adding memory:
- no-memory plan gives generic triage
- memory plan ranks likely root causes from similar incidents
- mitigation steps are prioritized by historical resolution success
- confidence is shown as a measurable memory boost
